About

Hi, I'm Sarah.
I make things make sense.

[ your photo here ]

I've always been the person in the room who notices what's missing — the follow-up no one sent, the table that needs one more chair, the drawer that's one organizer away from working perfectly. That instinct is what I've turned into a career.

Smart of Sorts is my small organizing practice, built around the idea that a well-ordered space or event isn't a luxury — it's what lets everything else run smoothly. I work with individuals, households, and event hosts to bring calm to the clutter.

When I'm not rearranging someone's kitchen or building a seating chart, you can find me in Kansas City with my family, probably making a list about something.
